2
Right After Release
0–2 hrs ★Most Critical
⚡ This is the most critical phase

Must Do (in order)

  • Click immediately from the notification and watch without leaving
  • Watch the full video from start to finish
  • Don't drop off early (first 30 seconds are critical)
  • Leave a text comment (specific reactions are great)
  • Press Like
Clicking from a notification directly counts toward CTR, which positively impacts the YouTube algorithm. If you missed it, please search manually and play the video.

NG — Do NOT do these

  • Muted & idle
  • Stopping or leaving midway
  • Looping on the same account
  • Simultaneous playback on the same account
  • Background play / split-screen play
  • Repeatedly fast-forwarding or pausing
  • Spamming emoji-only comments
Right after release, YouTube measures CTR × watch retention to decide whether to promote. Higher scores lead to wider distribution.

FAQ

  • Skipping ads is fine (no impact on view count)
  • But ad blockers are NG (may prevent views from being counted)
  • To rewatch, watch 3–4 other videos first and wait 10+ min before replaying
  • Always search manually to access each time
  • "Premium = unlimited replays" is a myth (repeated plays still won't count)
  • "Any replays within 24hrs is fine" is a myth (rapid replays are unlikely to count)

4
Sumin Party
Timing matters

When it works

  • Focus it right after release
  • Actually watch properly (full playback)
  • Comment & like at the same time
  • Right after a re-buzz or related event

When it doesn't work

  • Days after release (outside initial phase)
  • Looping / passive watching
  • Only existing fans participating

Playlist Usage

  • Playlists are great for background play & extending sessions
  • Use as a path to continued viewing after the initial phase
  • Playlist-driven views tend to extend watch sessions
Playlists aren't ideal for the initial boost, but they're great for building a habit of background play and rewatching over time.

Replay Intervals

  • Insert 3–4 other videos between replays
  • Leave a ~10 min gap between replays
A sumin party is a tool to maximize early momentum. Concentrating it right after release is key — simple replay loops days later have little effect.
